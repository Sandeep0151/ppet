Your Pet's Health is Our Priority-Ride With P-Pet Arrivals.

At P-Pet Arrivals, we understand that your furry companions are more than just pet - they are cherished members of your family. Based in the heart of Winnipeg, we specialized in providing safe, reliable and compassionate transportation service for pets to and from veterinary clinics, dog parks, grooming and boarding facilities.Our Mission is to bridge the gap for pet owners who, due to various reasons, are unable to personally escort their pets to essential appointments or activities.

Additional Services:

Veterinary Clinic Visits:

Regular check-ups, vaccinations, and consultations are vital for your pet's well-being. We transport your pet to and from veterinary clinics, ensuring they receive the necessary medical care.

Dog Park Excursions:

Exercise and socialization are essential for a dog's physical and mental health. We arrange fun outings to local dog parks, allowing your pet to interact, play, and expend their energy under supervision.

Grooming Spa Trips:

Pamper your pet with grooming sessions without the hassle of transportation. We safely escort your pet to grooming facilities, ensuring they return home looking and feeling their best.

Boarding Facility Transfers:

Whether you're traveling or need temporary pet care, we facilitate smooth transfers to and from boarding facilities, ensuring your pet stays in a secure and comfortable environment.
